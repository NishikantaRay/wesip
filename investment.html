Service Details

Odisha is emerging as a potential hub for new investments because of its geographic advantage and mineral reserves. We help new investments from their pre-project stage till they successfully commission and start operational activities. We majorly provide support in five key areas, pre & post project approvals, socio economic survey, social impact assessment, EC & FC processes and sustainable community engagement in the state of Odisha.
  • Conceptualization Services: Collaborative ideation to transform your investment concept into a viable and strategic plan.
  • Feasibility Studies: Thorough analysis to assess the economic, technical, and operational feasibility of the proposed investment.
  • Financial Modeling: Developing comprehensive financial models to project returns, risks, and potential outcomes throughout the investment lifecycle.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Navigating complex regulatory landscapes including Environmental and Forest Clearance to ensure compliance at every stage of the investment process.
  • Stakeholder engagements: Engaging government, public, NGOs, local communities etc. that includes planning & coordinating public hearings, facilitating Grama Sabhas and seamless acquisition of land.
  • Market Research and Analysis: In-depth market studies to identify opportunities, assess demand, and position the investment for success.
  • Risk Assessment and Mitigation: Identifying potential risks and implementing proactive strategies to mitigate challenges and uncertainties.
  • Investor Relations: Building and maintaining relationships with potential investors, providing clear communication and fostering trust.
  • Project Management: Efficiently overseeing and coordinating all aspects of the investment project from planning to execution.
  • Resource Procurement: Facilitating the acquisition of necessary resources, be it technology, materials, or skilled labor.
  • Installation and Commissioning: Hands-on support for the physical implementation of the investment, ensuring a smooth transition from planning to operational success.
