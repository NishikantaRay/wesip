Service Details

Odisha is emerging as a potential hub for new investments because of its geographic advantage and mineral reserves. We help new investments from their pre-project stage till they successfully commission and start operational activities. We majorly provide support in five key areas, pre & post project approvals, socio economic survey, social impact assessment, EC & FC processes and sustainable community engagement in the state of Odisha.

We recruit high-level professionals for key leadership positions with elaborate understanding of the organizations culture and aspiration we work with. Our proven process includes identifying, evaluating, and attracting candidates who possess the necessary skills, attitude and experience. We constantly endeavour to find candidates who align with the client company's strategic goals and can contribute to its success

We specialize in connecting you with top-tier talent for temporary, permanent, and contract positions. We offer outstanding staffing solutions involving a comprehensive approach to talent acquisition right from understanding the client's specific needs, and conducting thorough candidate assessments to providing a seamless recruitment process. We support organisations with complete functional staffing managing all aspects of those employees’ life cycle.

Our stellar HR Tech solutions “People Better” encompass cutting-edge technologies including AI to streamline human resource processes involving advanced tools for recruitment, employee management, performance evaluation, and workforce analytics. From applicant tracking systems to comprehensive HR information systems, we offer tools that enhance efficiency, automate tasks, and enable data-driven decision-making.

Our unique consulting stands out by tailoring its approach to each client's specific needs so as to align seamlessly with the business objectives; be it the transformation of an individual or an entity. It involves in-depth analysis, creative problem-solving, and a personalized strategy that goes beyond onesize-fits-all solutions. Our expert consultants, many of whom were corporate luminaries, leverage innovative thinking, industry expertise, and a deep understanding of client goals to provide distinctive and valuable insights.
